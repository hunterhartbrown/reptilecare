About ReptileCare

We publish reptile husbandry guidance that puts research first. We synthesize peer‑reviewed studies, field reports, standards from herpetology groups, and credible keeper data.

  • Evidence synthesis. We compare sources and note strength of evidence.
  • Citation hygiene. Every claim traces to a source you can check.
  • Clarity and readability. Short sections. Plain language. Actionable steps.

Ethics & Welfare Standards

We advocate captive‑bred animals, sustainable practices, and housing that exceeds bare minimums. UVB and heat are sized to species needs. Enclosures prioritize space, clean gradients, and enrichment that encourages natural behavior.

How We Build Knowledge

We plan to review community‑submitted care guides and include approved work in our library. Submissions will require sources and editorial review. This is a roadmap item; there is no submission form yet.

FAQ

Where do your recommendations come from?

We synthesize peer‑reviewed research, field studies, and credible husbandry standards. When evidence is mixed, we note it clearly.

Will you accept user guides?

Yes, in the future. We will review submissions for sourcing, clarity, and welfare standards before publishing.

Do you take sponsorships?

We keep recommendations independent and source‑driven. Products are chosen for fit with welfare standards.

How much keeper experience do you have?

Our focus is research synthesis over anecdotes. Personal keeper time is a small part of our process.
