Leopard Gecko Care Guide

Leopard Geckos are one of the most popular pet reptiles due to their gentle nature and ease of care. Learn everything you need to know to keep your gecko happy and healthy.

Species Info

Leopard Geckos are a species of gecko found in the deserts of Asia. They are known for their distinctive spots and gentle nature.

Habitat Size Requirements

Leopard Geckos need a habitat that is at least 10 gallons in size for a single gecko. More space is needed if housing multiple geckos.

Heating Requirements

Humidity / Airflow Requirements

Leopard Geckos thrive in low humidity environments, typically around 30-40%. Ensure adequate ventilation to prevent respiratory issues.

Food Requirements

Leopard Geckos primarily eat insects such as crickets, mealworms, and dubia roaches. Dust their food with calcium and vitamin D3 supplements.

Opportunities for Enrichment

Provide hiding spots, climbing structures, and varying substrates to keep your Leopard Gecko engaged and comfortable.
